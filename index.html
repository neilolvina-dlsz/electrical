Pro Circuit Tips

  • A Battery stores chemical energy & creates a potential difference to push electrons.
  • A closed Switch forms a continuous, unbroken path of low resistance, permitting electric charges to drift.
  • A Resistor acts like a bottleneck, restricting charge flow to protect sensitive components from overheating.
  • A conventional Current Flow model goes from the positive terminal (+) to the negative terminal (-).

Component Cheat Sheet

Light Bulb (Lamp)

An electrical load that transforms electrical current energy directly into radiant light energy.

Switch (Open)

An easy trigger to sever the path of the circuit loop, immediately halting electron flow.

Battery / Cell

Combines chemical actions to deliver continuous electromotive voltage potential.

Resistor

Specifically designed to hinder flow intensity of electrical current (measured in Ohms).
